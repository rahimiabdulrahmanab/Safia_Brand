Our Founder & Story

A Vision Born
from Afghan Soil

Afghanistan has long been a land of extraordinary natural abundance. Its saffron fields, fig orchards, apricot gardens, and mountain pine forests produce some of the most treasured foods on earth — yet for generations, this quality went largely unknown to the world.

Safia was founded with a singular purpose: to connect Afghanistan's finest produce directly with global buyers. We believe Afghan farmers deserve dignified access to international markets — and that buyers around the world deserve produce of real, traceable provenance.

Eng Sanaullah Aftab
Founder & Director — Safia Brand, Kabul
